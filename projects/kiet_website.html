About KIET

KIET Group of Institutions, Ghaziabad, is one of the premier technical institutions in Northern India. Established in 1998, KIET has been dedicated to providing quality education in engineering, pharmacy, and management, fostering innovation, and promoting research. We strive to create technically competent professionals and responsible citizens who can contribute positively to society.

Our Academic Departments

Information Technology (IT)

Focusing on software development, data management, networking, and modern IT solutions. Preparing students for the digital age.

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Core computer science principles, algorithms, programming, and system design for robust software and hardware solutions.

CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

Specialized program in AI and Machine Learning, covering deep learning, natural language processing, and data science.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Exploring electronics, telecommunications, signal processing, and integrated circuit design for advanced communication systems.

Mechanical Engineering

Designing, analyzing, manufacturing, and maintaining mechanical systems using principles of physics and materials science.

Civil Engineering

Planning, designing, and managing infrastructure projects like roads, bridges, buildings, and water systems.
